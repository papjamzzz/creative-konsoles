How it works

Parallel in. Synthesis out.

Most people pick one AI and hope it's right. 5i treats model disagreement as data — the shape of the disagreement tells you something the individual answers don't.

Step 01

You send one prompt

Type once. 5i fires it to all five providers simultaneously — one API call per model, all in parallel.

Step 02

Five responses come back

Each model answers in full. Read every response individually or skip straight to the synthesis.

Step 03

One verdict emerges

A synthesis pass weighs all five answers into a single conclusion — surfacing consensus and mapping disagreements.

Who it's for

For decisions where getting it wrong costs something

One AI can be confidently wrong. Five AIs disagreeing tells you the question is harder than it looks. Five AIs agreeing gives you something closer to ground truth.

5i is used for high-stakes research, investment thesis validation, legal analysis, technical architecture decisions, and any prompt where a single model's blind spots could cost you. If the answer matters, run it through all five.
