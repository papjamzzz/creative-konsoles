FAQ

What people usually ask.

What platforms do you build for?
Primarily web apps (Python/Flask), macOS desktop tools, and automation scripts. Can also handle iOS/Android if the scope warrants it. If it runs on a computer, I can probably build it.
Do I need a technical background to work with you?
No. Describe it how you'd explain it to someone who isn't a developer. The vaguer the better — I'll ask the right questions to get to a tight spec.
Who actually builds it?
Me. One person. No outsourcing, no team of contractors. This is why it's fast and cheap and you can actually talk to the person writing your code.
What's the payment structure?
50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Nothing changes hands until we've agreed on a scope, price, and timeline.
Do I get the source code?
Always. You own everything I build for you — code, assets, all of it. I don't retain rights or hold your build hostage behind a subscription.
What if I need changes after delivery?
Bug fixes are covered for 30 days at no cost. Feature additions after delivery are scoped as a new project — usually discounted since we're already in sync.
